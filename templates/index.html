Early Detection is Critical

Leprosy (Hansen's Disease) is a chronic infectious disease caused by Mycobacterium leprae. Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial to prevent disability and reduce transmission.

Our AI system can detect early signs of leprosy that might be missed by visual examination alone. The application uses Explainable AI to help users understand which skin features triggered the detection.
